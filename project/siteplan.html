Ghana Travel Guide – Site Plan

Your trusted guide to exploring Ghana

Site Name

Ghana Travel Guide

This name clearly communicates the purpose of the website: to help visitors explore Ghana’s culture, food, attractions, and travel opportunities.

Optional domain: ghanatravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide reliable, easy‑to‑understand information about traveling in Ghana. Visitors will learn about top destinations, cultural highlights, food recommendations, and travel tips. A simple contact form will allow users to reach out for help or ask questions.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist attractions to visit in Ghana?
  • Where can I find authentic Ghanaian food when I visit?
  • How do I contact a local guide for help?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #C8102E (Ghana Red) – used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #006B3F (Ghana Green) – used for buttons and highlights.

Neutral Color: #F2F2F2 – used for background sections.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat – used for all headings.

Body Font: Open Sans – used for paragraphs and general content.
